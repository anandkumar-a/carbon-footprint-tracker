About EcoGuide

EcoGuide is designed as an AI Sustainability Coach that helps you understand carbon sources, simulate better choices, and build a practical action plan.

What EcoGuide does
  • Analyzes your daily habits
  • Identifies the highest emission sources
  • Offers prioritized improvement suggestions
  • Lets you simulate lifestyle changes
  • Tracks progress with simple actions

EcoGuide is built with Flask, SQLite, Chart.js, and modular logic so you can expand it with more AI features and real-time sustainability integrations.

Why it matters

Small daily choices have a lasting climate impact. EcoGuide turns those choices into clear actions and measurable progress.
